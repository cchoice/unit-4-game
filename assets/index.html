Crystal Collector Game

How to Play!

You will be given a random number in the yellow box at the start of the game.

Try to figure out the amount of points each crystal is worth by clicking on it. Click on the crystals until your score equals the amount of points in the yellow box.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the number in the yellow box.
